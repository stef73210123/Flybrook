Our Story

Inspired. Seasonal. Organic. Local. Fresh.

A new American restaurant, bar and gathering place in the heart of downtown Armonk, New York.

Flybrook raises the bar in Westchester hospitality — incredible ingredients, a seasonally distinguished dining experience, and a dynamic bar at the heart of Armonk. We source from our own 117-acre regenerative organic farm in Livingston Manor and small family farms across the Catskills and Hudson Valley — a short, honest distance from field to table.

Executive Chef

Roxanne Spruance

A critically acclaimed chef based in New York City and the Hudson Valley, Roxanne is known for innovative, seasonal cooking that respects and incorporates regional traditions.

At Flybrook she serves lunch and dinner built around ingredients grown on our own land and across the valley — a calm, biophilic room where the food and the land do the talking.

The name

It draws on two places at once.

The fishing place between the hills.

Armonck is what the Lenape called this land. Our restaurant sits in the heart of downtown Armonk, a few blocks from where that water still runs.

The home of the hand-tied fly.

Livingston Manor, where our farm sits, is the storied home of American fly fishing. The brook, the hatch, the dry fly — our mark honors water, patience, and the land that feeds the kitchen.
